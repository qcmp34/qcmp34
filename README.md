# Quantum Computing Projects Portfolio

Welcome! This repository showcases my journey through various quantum computing competitions, hackathons, and research programs. Below you'll find a collection of projects spanning quantum algorithms, quantum machine learning, and quantum simulation.

## üéØ Featured Projects

### 1. [Classiq AztecHacks 2024](https://github.com/qcmp34/Classiq.aztechacks2024)
**Competition:** AztecHacks 2024 - SDSU Quantum Computing Club  
**Duration:** April 24-28, 2024  
**Technology:** Classiq SDK

A competitive hackathon project featuring the implementation of 10 progressively difficult quantum computing challenges using the Classiq platform. This project demonstrates proficiency in quantum algorithm design and implementation.

**Key Achievements:**
- Implemented quantum arithmetic functions and algorithms
- Developed GHZ state generation circuits
- Created quantum implementations of mathematical functions including hyperbolic tangent approximations
- Worked with quantum circuit synthesis and optimization

**Technologies:** Classiq SDK, Quantum Circuit Design, Python, Jupyter Notebooks

### 2. [Quantum Galton Board - Womanium 2025](https://github.com/qcmp34/Womanium2025--QuantumGaltonBoard)
**Program:** Womanium & WISER Quantum Program 2025  
**Partner:** U.S. Naval Nuclear Laboratory  
**Focus:** Quantum Walks & Monte Carlo Methods

Implementation of a noise-aware quantum Galton Board - a quantum circuit analogue of the classical bean machine that demonstrates quantum random walks and produces various statistical distributions.

**Key Features:**
- Modular circuit generator for arbitrary layer counts
- Multiple encoding strategies (one-hot CSWAP vs. binary encoding)
- Noise model simulations and transpiler optimizations
- Statistical distribution analysis (Gaussian, exponential, quantum walk variants)
- Verification under ideal and noisy conditions

**Technologies:** Qiskit, PennyLane, Quantum Fourier Transform, NISQ-era optimization

**Research Applications:**
- Universal statistical simulators
- Quantum Monte Carlo methods
- Particle transport modeling
- High-dimensional problem solving

### 3. [Development of Novel Quantum Algorithms](https://github.com/qcmp34/Development-of-Novel-Quantum-Algorithms)
**Program:** Womanium Quantum+AI 2024  
**Partner:** Classiq  
**Focus:** Quantum Algorithms with Exponential Advantages

Exploration and implementation of advanced quantum algorithms that offer exponential speedups over classical counterparts. This project focuses on practical applications of state-of-the-art quantum computing techniques.

**Core Algorithms Covered:**
- **Shor's Algorithm** - Quantum factorization for cryptography applications
- **HHL Algorithm** - Quantum linear system solver
- **Hamiltonian Simulation** - Quantum system evolution
- **Variational Quantum Algorithms** - Hybrid quantum-classical optimization

**Project Scope:**
- Theoretical understanding and implementation
- Real-world application scenarios
- Performance analysis and optimization
- Hardware-agnostic algorithm design

**Technologies:** Classiq Platform, Quantum Circuit Synthesis, Advanced Algorithm Design

---

### 4. [QML for Conspicuity Detection in Production](https://github.com/qcmp34/QML-for-Conspicuity-Detection-in-Production)
**Program:** Womanium Quantum+AI 2024  
**Partner:** Fraunhofer Institute (ITWM)  
**Focus:** Quantum Machine Learning for Manufacturing

Application of hybrid quantum-classical computing to accelerate production process optimization through early identification of improvement measures and anomaly detection.

**Project Objectives:**
- Implement Quantum Machine Learning models for manufacturing data
- Develop Quantum Neural Networks (QNNs) for pattern recognition
- Apply Quantum Convolutional Neural Networks (QCNNs) for image analysis
- Create Variational Quantum Classifiers for anomaly detection

**Use Cases:**
- Production process optimization
- Quality control and defect detection
- Time series analysis of manufacturing data
- Image-based conspicuity detection
- Early identification of improvement opportunities

**Technologies:** PennyLane, PyTorch, Quantum Machine Learning, Hybrid Quantum-Classical Models

**Impact:** Accelerated data analysis for production optimization, enabling faster identification of quality issues and process improvements.
